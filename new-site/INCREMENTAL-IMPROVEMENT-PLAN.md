## NOTES & PRINCIPLES

### Keep In Mind
‚úÖ **Authentic**: Everything must feel genuine, no hype
‚úÖ **Professional**: Board-level audience demands sophistication
‚úÖ **Clear**: No jargon, no complexity - straight talk
‚úÖ **Evidence-Based**: Back claims with credentials, results, testimonials
‚úÖ **User-Focused**: What does the visitor need to know/feel/do?

### Avoid
‚ùå **Overcomplicated**: Keep it clean and navigable
‚ùå **Generic Stock Content**: Every word must be specific to Craig
‚ùå **Fear-Based Selling**: No scare tactics or negativity
