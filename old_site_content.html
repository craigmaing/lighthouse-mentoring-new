Psychological Safety Audit FAQ

Common questions about workplace wellbeing audits, pricing, and our Human+AI methodology

A psychological safety audit is a comprehensive workplace mental health assessment that evaluates how safe employees feel to speak up, ask questions, and admit mistakes without fear of punishment. Our Human+AI approach combines traditional survey methods with advanced AI analysis to identify patterns and provide deeper insights into workplace culture and employee wellbeing.

Our transparent wellbeing audit pricing ranges from £18-30 per employee and includes: anonymous employee surveys, AI-powered pattern analysis, comprehensive written report with actionable recommendations, one-hour consultation call, and 3-month follow-up support. No hidden costs - everything is included in our per-employee fee.

Our unique Human+AI methodology combines Craig Fearn's FRSPH expertise with advanced AI analysis. Human insight designs the assessment framework and interprets cultural nuances, while AI processes large datasets to identify hidden patterns in employee responses. This combination provides deeper insights than traditional workplace wellbeing consultants can deliver alone.

Absolutely. All employee wellbeing assessments are completely anonymous and GDPR compliant. Individual responses are never shared - only aggregated insights and patterns are reported. Our ISO 45003 compliant process ensures the highest standards of data protection and employee confidentiality throughout the entire audit process.

ISO 45003 is the international standard for managing psychological health and safety at work. As an ISO 45003 consultant, Craig ensures our workplace mental health assessments meet the highest international standards. This compliance demonstrates your organization's commitment to employee wellbeing and helps protect against legal and reputational risks.

A typical psychological safety audit takes 2-3 weeks from launch to final report delivery. Employee surveys run for 7-10 days to ensure maximum participation, followed by 5-7 days for AI analysis and human interpretation. Rush services are available for urgent workplace wellbeing concerns with 48-72 hour turnaround for preliminary insights.

We specialize in UK SMEs with 50-1000 employees. This sweet spot allows us to provide personalized attention while leveraging AI for meaningful pattern recognition. Smaller teams benefit from more detailed individual insights, while larger organizations gain from sophisticated statistical analysis of workplace culture trends.

Traditional consultants rely solely on human interpretation, which can miss subtle patterns in large datasets. Our Human+AI approach combines the best of both: Craig's FRSPH expertise ensures cultural sensitivity and professional insight, while AI analysis reveals hidden correlations and trends that humans alone might miss. You get deeper insights at lower cost than traditional consulting firms.
