## ABOUT SECTION (1,847 characters / 2,600 max)

```
I help board-level executives and senior leaders build resilient, high-performing organisations through strategic coaching, board advisory, and evidence-based wellbeing governance. With dual fellowships (FRSPH, FCMI) and serving as IoD Wellbeing Ambassador, I bring both strategic expertise and deep empathy to leadership development.

My Background

After two decades working at senior levels across education, healthcare, and organisational development, I founded Lighthouse Mentoring to address a gap I'd seen repeatedly: brilliant leaders struggling with the human dimensions of organisational performance. Too often, wellbeing is treated as a compliance issue rather than a strategic advantage. I knew there was a better way.

What Makes My Approach Different

Unlike traditional coaching or advisory models, I integrate:
• Board-level perspective from NED experience and IoD Ambassador role
• Evidence-based frameworks backed by research (Yale Science of Well-Being, Johns Hopkins Psychological First Aid)
• Human+AI methodology combining empathetic coaching with data-driven wellbeing audits
• Holistic integration of leadership effectiveness and organisational wellbeing
• Governance lens on wellbeing strategy and accountability

The Impact

My clients consistently report:
• Significantly improved leadership effectiveness and executive presence
• Enhanced board performance through clearer governance frameworks
• Measurable improvements in organisational wellbeing and culture
• Better strategic decision-making under pressure
• Increased resilience and sustainable high performance

Credentials & Expertise

• FRSPH Fellow (Royal Society for Public Health) – Public health and wellbeing expertise
• FCMI Fellow (Chartered Management Institute) – Management and leadership excellence
• IoD Ambassador (Institute of Directors South West) – Board governance and wellbeing advocacy
• EMCC Professional Coach – Ethical coaching standards
• MEd, BSc (Hons), PGCE (L7), QTLS – Educational and professional qualifications
• Recent certifications: Yale Science of Well-Being, Johns Hopkins Psychological First Aid, NEDonBoard NED Accelerator, UC Davis Health Managing as a Coach

Beyond Coaching & Advisory

I'm passionate about making workplace wellbeing a board-level strategic priority, not just an HR initiative. I regularly speak on governance, leadership resilience, and the business case for organisational wellbeing.

Let's Connect
```
