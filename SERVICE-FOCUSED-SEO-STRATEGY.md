# Service-Focused SEO Strategy - DataForSEO Research Results

**Date:** October 7, 2025
**Objective:** Complete content strategy rewrite targeting SERVICE BUYERS, not educational researchers
**Methodology:** DataForSEO keyword research, SERP competitor analysis, buyer-intent mapping

---

## Executive Summary

**Critical Finding:** Current content strategy attracts wrong audience (job seekers, researchers) instead of service buyers (current board members, CEOs with problems).

**Strategic Pivot:** Shift from educational content ("how to become a NED") to problem-solving content ("how to fix board dysfunction") that positions Craig as the solution.

**Data-Driven Approach:** All recommendations based on DataForSEO search volume, CPC (commercial intent), and competition analysis.
